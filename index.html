Welcome to TransformEXL Ai!

TransformEXL Ai, powered by advanced language models like Gemini, is designed to revolutionize how you interact with and extract insights from your Excel data. We understand that spreadsheets can hold vast amounts of information, often in complex structures. Our platform intelligently processes your Excel files, identifies tabular data (even multiple tables across sheets or within a single sheet), and prepares it for powerful analysis, querying, and visualization.

Whether you're dealing with sales figures, operational data, financial reports, or any other structured information in Excel, TransformEXL Ai aims to simplify your workflow, automate tedious data preparation tasks, and unlock the stories hidden within your spreadsheets.

This guide will walk you through the initial setup and provide best practices for using the platform effectively.

Most Important: Access & Permissions

By default, new users will not have permission to upload data or access workspaces. To get started, please request permission by emailing us at example@gmail.com.

You will typically receive access to a dedicated workspace within 24–48 hours.

Setup & Guidance Steps

  1. File Uploads

    You can upload multiple Excel files (.xlsx, .xls).

    The maximum file size is currently 50 MB per file.

    Tip: For larger datasets, consider splitting them into smaller files or ensure your connection is stable during upload.

  2. Processing Time

    After uploading, please allow some time for processing:

    • Small files (under 5 MB): Typically ~30 seconds to 1 minute.
    • Larger files (up to 50 MB): May take a few minutes.

    Processing involves data extraction, table detection, and schema inference.

  3. Supported Data Structures

    TransformEXL Ai supports:

    • Single table per sheet: Standard Excel format.
    • Multiple sheets per file: Each sheet can contain its own tables.
    • Multiple tables in one sheet: Separate with empty rows/columns.

    Reference images below illustrate these formats.

    Best Practice: Cleaner layouts yield faster, more accurate results.

  4. Unsupported Content

    Please avoid uploading files that contain:

    • Embedded images or charts (these will be ignored).
    • Complex macros (they won’t execute).
    • Password-protected files (remove protection first).

  5. Beta Program & Feedback

    Beta access is free. We value your feedback on features, usability, and any issues.

    Significant contributors may receive special perks as our thanks!

    Thank you for helping us improve!
