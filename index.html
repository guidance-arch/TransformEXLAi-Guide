Welcome to TransformEXL AI

TransformEXL AI is designed to revolutionize how you interact with and extract insights from your data. We understand that spreadsheets can hold vast amounts of information in complex structures. Our platform intelligently processes your spreadsheet files, identifies tabular data (even multiple tables across sheets or within a single sheet), and prepares it for powerful analysis, easy querying, and visualization.

Whether you're dealing with sales figures, operational data, financial reports, or any other structured information in a spreadsheet format, TransformEXL AI aims to simplify your workflow, automate tedious data preparation tasks, and unlock the stories hidden within your spreadsheets.

This guide will walk you through the initial setup and provide best practices for using the platform effectively.
